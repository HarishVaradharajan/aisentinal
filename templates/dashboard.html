📌 About the Project

AI Sentinel is an AI-powered web application designed to assist users in understanding legal concepts and predicting crime-related incidents based on their queries. By leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP), the system interprets user input and maps it to relevant Indian laws, providing accurate and meaningful insights.

  • 🔍 Smart Query Understanding: similarity to match user queries with legal data.
  • ⚖️ Law Lookup: Matches relevant IPC sections and other laws based on your input.
  • 📊 Live Query Stats: Tracks crime prediction and query volume in real-time.
